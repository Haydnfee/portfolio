Process and Techniques

With the base design made up in illustrator including lines and basic color, I then brought that into Photoshop. Nothing fancy is needed to be done in Illustrator, really I could have just done the line work but adding color doesnt take much time and makes everything else a bit easier. Everything in photoshop is now just done with custom brushes. Using the Illistrator file, i then simple select portions of the image and splash color in the area. Instead of sharp detail, the same technique is used to show things like shadow on the helmet, or blood on the lance.

Why

This is based off the MMO "Final Fantasy XIV" and one of it's classes "Dragoon". I wanted to start making little things like this to represent all of the classes in the game. It also gives me a chance to experiment with negative space to replace color and solid edges.

This page will be periodically updated with more as I casually make them.
