Process and Techniques

As this is a logo for NFL player, Odell Beckham Jr, the obvious route is to include his initials into the logo. Nothing weird went into making this, it's simply trying to include all 3 letters into the design while not looking like a mess of shapes ontop of more shapes.

Why

This was a concept logo for one of my college projects. The task was to create a website for and rebrand an NFL player. Our team took on rebranding Odell Beckham Jr. This was not the final logo as it was a bit more abstract then what we were looking for.
