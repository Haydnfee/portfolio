Process and Techniques

I started with a smoothe, sans serif font and began to play around with the shapes and connecting various letters till i had something that resembled an edgy 80s "vaporwave" aesthetic. For colors i decided on a muted neon tones, taking bright, vibrant pinks and blues and toning down their intesity just a bit. I wanted the "7" to pop out from the rest of the text so a metal palette gradient was fitting. Once i added a simple geometric shape, i converted to entire logo to 3d with Illustrators native 3d renderer.

Why

The origin of this idea was way back early in high school. It was more of a joke more then anything. My friend would always joke about starting a synth hairmetal band named "The Neon Pink 7s". Turns out making a logo for it was a fun idea.
